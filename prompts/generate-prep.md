# Generate Interview Prep

Use the private job folder as the only source of truth.

Inputs:

- role description
- company notes
- resume or profile notes
- user-provided priorities

Output:

- role thesis
- likely interviewer priorities
- strongest relevant proof
- gaps and honest ways to handle them
- questions to ask
- 30/60/90-day angle if useful

Do not invent experience, company facts, or metrics.
